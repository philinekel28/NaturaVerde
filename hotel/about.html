VISION and MISSION of Natura Verde

VISION

The Natura Verde envisions itself to be the premier boutie hotel in the Baguio City by providing affordable acoomodation, delectable Filipino and Italian cuisine and succulent beverages through splendid service beyond guest expectation.

MISSION
The Natura Verde aims to:
  • offer a great accomodation with an eco-friendly ambiance and first class amenities;
  • serve mouthwatering cuisine in casual fine ding experience; and
  • provide an outstanding service for extraordinary experience

Objective of Natura Verde

The Natura Verde commits to:
  • continuously innovate the facilities and services to answer the needs of its dinamic market;
  • continually offer healthy choices of food by making use of fresh and organic ingredients; and
  • endlessly develop and maintain a staff capable of making every stay of the guests with the hotel always a worthy experience.
